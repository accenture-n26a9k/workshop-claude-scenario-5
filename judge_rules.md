The Judging
Claude does the judging. Top 5 teams present live, 5 minutes each.

What definitely gets read

README.md — Tells the story. What you built, what's next.
presentation.html — Your 5-minute pitch, built with Claude Code.
CLAUDE.md — How you taught the tool to work your way.

If Claude only sees these three, it should still understand what you built. Don't leave them to the last ten minutes.
